Tom Yung Goong: A Spicy Thai Delight

Tom Yum Goong is a classic Thai soup renowned for its bold flavors and aromatic spices. This hot
and sour soup features a tantalizing blend lemongrass, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves,
combined with succelent shrimp.

The key to a delicious Tom Yung Goong lies in the balance of flavors--sweet, sour,
salty and spicy.

Ingredients

  • Lemongrass: 2 stalks
  • Thai Basil: 1 cup
  • Kaffir lime leaves: 3 Leaves
  • Shrimp: 500g

Preparation

  1. Simmer the broth for 10 minutes.
  2. Add lemongrass, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves.
  3. Stir the shrimp and cook until its pink.
  4. season with fish sauce, lime juice, and chili paste.
  5. Garnish with thai basil and serve hot.

Cooking Tips

  • 1/2 cup of fish sauce adds authentic Thai flavor.
  • 1 tablespoon of chili paste gives the soup its signature heat.
